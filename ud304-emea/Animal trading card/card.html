Polar Bear

Polar Bear's skin is black for absorbing energy from the sun and the fur hairs are transparent.

  • Scientific Name: Ursus maritimus
  • Average Lenght: 2,4 metres
  • Average Lifespan: 30 years
  • Habitat: Ice covered waters

The polar bear travels long distances over vast desolate expanses, generally on drifting oceanic ice floes, searching for seals, its primary prey. The polar bear is one of the largest and most powerful carnivore on land.
